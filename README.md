Problem Statement

Most mainstream fitness apps produce generic workout and diet plans that fail to consider individual differences, cultural food patterns, available equipment, or financial limitations. Students especially need fitness guidance that is adaptable, budget-friendly, and realistic to their lifestyle.

Project Overview

This project introduces a Personalized Workout & Diet Planner with AI, which generates customized exercise routines and meal plans tailored to user preferences and constraints. By leveraging AI, the system enhances practicality and accessibility while promoting healthy habits for students.

Solution Highlights

1.  Personalized workout routines
2.  AI-generated meal plans
3.  Budget-conscious options
4.  Cultural food preference support
5.  Student lifestyle optimization
6.  Effective and achievable fitness goals
